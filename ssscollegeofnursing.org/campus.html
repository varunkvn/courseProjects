CAMPUS

           Sri Sai Saran College of Nursing is located in its own spacious land with creamy white buildings surrounded by lush green lawns and hanging gardens with natural flora and fauna. The campus is a boon to the students to enjoy the nature with a variety of trees and birds. Games both indoor and outdoor are provided are provided for the physical growth and to sustain mental hygiene. The college has well ventilated class rooms for the students. To enhance the presentation of seminars,lectures each class is equipped with a overhead projector. The institute has a good audio visual setup with LCD projector,sound system to improve classroom acoustics,various models and charts for use by faculty and students.
