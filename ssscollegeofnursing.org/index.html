INTRODUCTION

      The Education Society constituted in 1964 started with humble beginning by starting Bhagawan Sri Sai Baba High School(T.M) and then started E.M High School in the name of Sri Sai Baba. Later the Society realizing the necessity of neglected poor,down trodden and sickly people brooded over the matter of optimum health care, started drawing from all around the society started Sri Sai Saran College of Nursing, Morampudi,education,Rajahmundry from the year 2006-2007 to provide excellent Nursing eduction realizing the increased Para medical demand at home and out side the nation. Today you all know that several challenges are thrown open for medical care and as a result increased demand for Nursing Education necessitated.

      The College is affiliated to N.T.R University of Health Sciences, Vijayawada. It is recognized by the Andhra Pradesh Nurse's & Midwive's council and Indian Nursing Council, New Delhi.

      The BSc Nursing Programme is a four year Degree Programme including six months Internship. Admission of the students shall be once in year. The annual intake is 50 students

      The revised curriculum based on INC (Indian Nursing Council) syllabus has been implemented from the academic year 2006- 2007. An all-round academic and clinical experience is offered through classroom teaching, varied clinical experiences, conferences, health exhibitions, talks by eminent personalities and visits to various places to fulfil the requirement.
